Interesting Facts

  • Hip-hop music originated in the Bronx, New York City in the 1970s. It was influenced by funk, soul, and jazz music, and has since become a global phenomenon.

  • Notable Hip-Hop Artists

    • The Sugarhill Gang
    • Grandmaster Flash and the Furious Five
    • The Beastie Boys

Notable Artists

Hip-Hop

  • Kendrick Lamar is a critically acclaimed rapper and songwriter from Compton, California. He is known for his storytelling ability and socially conscious lyrics.

  • Cardi B is a rapper and singer from the Bronx, New York City. She is known for her chart-topping hits and outspoken personality.

Rock

  • Elvis Presley is often referred to as the "King of Rock and Roll." He was a singer, musician, and actor who is known for his charismatic stage presence and hit songs like "Hound Dog" and "Jailhouse Rock."

  • John Lennon was a singer, songwriter, and member of the Beatles. He is known for his activism and hit songs like "Imagine" and "Revolution."
